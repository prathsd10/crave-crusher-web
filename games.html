Why Games Help With Cravings

Distraction Works

Studies show that engaging your brain in an activity can help cravings pass. Most cravings last 3-5 minutes.

Dopamine Release

Games provide a small dopamine hit that can help replace what your brain craves when wanting a cigarette.

Brain Retraining

By consistently using alternative activities during cravings, you're teaching your brain new response patterns.
